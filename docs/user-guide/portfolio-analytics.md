# Portfolio Analytics

The Portfolio workspace connects to your IBKR account to provide analytics across your ETF positions.

## Requirements

- IBKR TWS or IB Gateway running with API enabled (see [IBKR Setup](ibkr-setup.md))

## Views

### Overview

Portfolio summary: total market value, cash, net liquidation, and position count.

### Positions

All current positions with market value, unrealized P&L, and portfolio weight.

### Lookthrough

Decomposes your ETF positions into their underlying holdings. Answers: "What do my ETFs actually make me own?"

Holdings from each ETF are weighted by your position size to show true underlying exposure.

### Exposure

Aggregated sector and geographic exposure across all portfolio ETFs, weighted by position size.

### Concentration

Portfolio-level concentration analysis. Shows how concentrated your combined holdings are at the underlying stock level.

### Margin

Margin utilization, buying power, maintenance margin, and leverage ratio. Highlights when leverage exceeds configured warning thresholds.

### Risk

Portfolio risk metrics including concentration risk, single-name exposure, and leverage warnings.
